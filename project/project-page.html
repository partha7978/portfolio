A modern to do application where you can do all the basic operations. It comes with dark theme as well as light theme and you can change your preference.
The functionality of this application are:

  • Add/delete your todos.
  • You can mark it as complete/incomplete for any of your tasks.
  • Toggle the theme according to your preference.
  • Local Storage feature also there :)
